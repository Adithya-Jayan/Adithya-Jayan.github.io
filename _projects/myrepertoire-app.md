### Architectural Overview & Purpose
This application is designed for musicians and performers to bridge the gap between learning and long-term retention. It serves as a digital companion for managing complex repertoires, ensuring that resources like PDFs, videos, and audio recordings are always at your fingertips.

### Key Features:
- **Repertoire Library:** Organize your pieces in flexible list or grid formats with deep metadata support.
- **Media Attachments:** Seamlessly attach and view PDFs, Markdown notes, local images, audio files, and external video links.
- **Practice Tracking:** Log every practice session to monitor progress over time with built-in history tracking.
- **Advanced Organization:** Utilize powerful search, multi-level filtering, and custom sorting capabilities to navigate large collections.
- **Data Integrity:** Reliable manual and automatic backup/restore options to ensure your practice data is never lost.

Developed with Flutter for cross-platform stability, MyRepertoire focuses on providing a fast, offline-first experience for artists on the go.
